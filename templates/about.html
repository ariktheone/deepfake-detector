About SurakshaNetra Platform

SurakshaNetra represents a breakthrough in deepfake detection technology, combining multiple AI models and advanced computer vision techniques to provide comprehensive analysis of video content.


Key Features:

  • Multi-model ensemble detection for enhanced accuracy
  • Real-time video processing and analysis
  • Advanced facial landmark and expression analysis
  • Temporal consistency validation across video frames
  • Industry-leading false positive reduction
  • Comprehensive reporting and visualization
  • Scalable cloud-based architecture

Our platform is designed to be accessible to both technical and non-technical users, providing detailed insights while maintaining an intuitive interface that makes digital security accessible to everyone.
