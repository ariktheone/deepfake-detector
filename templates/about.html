Mission & Vision

My Mission: To democratize deepfake detection technology and make it accessible to everyone, from content creators to cybersecurity professionals. SurakshaNetra represents my commitment to building the first line of defense against malicious deepfake content in our digital world.


My Vision: A future where digital authenticity is preserved, and people can trust the media they consume. Through advanced AI and machine learning, I'm building the essential tools needed to maintain trust in our increasingly digital society.


Why SurakshaNetra? The name "SurakshaNetra" combines Sanskrit words meaning "protective eye" - representing my dedication to safeguarding digital authenticity through intelligent visual analysis and cutting-edge AI technology.

About SurakshaNetra Platform

SurakshaNetra represents my breakthrough achievement in deepfake detection technology, combining multiple AI models and advanced computer vision techniques to provide comprehensive analysis of video content.


Key Features I've Developed:

  • Multi-Model Ensemble: Three specialized detectors working in parallel for maximum accuracy
  • Real-Time Processing: Fast analysis with detailed frame-by-frame results
  • Advanced AI: Custom-trained neural networks for facial analysis and temporal consistency
  • User-Friendly Interface: Professional design accessible to both technical and non-technical users
  • Comprehensive Reports: Detailed analysis with confidence scores and visual overlays
  • Privacy-First: Secure processing with no data retention policies
  • Cross-Platform: Works on any device with a modern web browser
  • Continuous Learning: AI models that improve over time with new data patterns

Built with modern web technologies and deployed with scalability in mind, SurakshaNetra is designed to be the go-to solution for anyone concerned about digital media authenticity. Every line of code reflects my commitment to excellence and innovation in AI security.
